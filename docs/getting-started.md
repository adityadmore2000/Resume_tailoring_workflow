# Getting Started

## What this does
Helps you move from a single “master resume” to repeatable, evidence-backed resume generation for multiple job descriptions.

## Why it exists
Most AI resume tools rewrite text without grounding. This platform retrieves *only* verified experience evidence from an Experience Bank before generating any bullets.

## Step-by-step usage
1. **Create an Experience Bank** (upload your master resume once).
2. **Preview the Experience Bank** (sanity-check extracted experience, projects, and evidence).
3. **Tailor a resume** (paste/upload a Job Description; select the bank).
4. **Review & export** in the **Resume Workspace** (edit LaTeX, recompile, export PDF).

## What happens internally
- Your master resume is parsed into structured sections and atomic evidence claims.
- A knowledge base (KB) is written and embedded into a per-bank vector index.
- Tailoring retrieves relevant chunks, verifies evidence, and assembles output from verified claims only.

## Common mistakes
- Creating a new bank for every job (you usually want **one stable bank** per “master resume”).
- Expecting missing skills to be invented (unsupported skills/tools are excluded by design).
- Skipping preview (you’ll get better outputs if the bank content is accurate first).

## Recommended next steps
- Create an Experience Bank → Preview it → Tailor your first role.
