# FAQ

## What this does
Answers common “why” questions while you use the workflow.

## Why it exists
This product is intentionally different from typical resume rewriters. These answers help you understand the constraints and how to get better outputs.

## Common questions

### Why do Experience Banks exist?
To avoid re-uploading and re-parsing resumes for every job, and to keep a stable source-of-truth for evidence retrieval.

### Why is resume upload not needed during tailoring?
Tailoring uses your selected Experience Bank + its vector index. Uploading the resume again would add noise and reduce repeatability.

### Why was a skill/tool excluded?
Because it wasn’t supported by verified evidence in your bank. The system doesn’t invent skills.

### What does “evidence-grounded” mean?
Generated content is assembled only from claims that can be traced back to the bank’s evidence.

### What is traceability?
An audit trail from generated bullets back to evidence IDs and source text.

## Common mistakes
- Expecting “magic” skills to be added without evidence.
- Using multiple banks for the same resume without a reason.

## Recommended next steps
- If something is missing, improve your Experience Bank (master resume → regenerate bank) rather than editing every tailored resume by hand.
